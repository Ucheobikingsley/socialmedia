AXA IWD Campaign

Brief: Following the theme, “Choose to challenge “ create a digital campaign for AXA to commemorate International Women‘s Day

IDEA: Beat the odds, Choose to Challenge. We live in a highly patriarchal society in which women are faced with societal and even some workplace culture that relegates them to the background. This reality has tainted the mindset of some women in our TA and has limited their aspirations. In line with the global theme; Choose to Challenge, to these women we are saying choose to challenge the status quo, and become the best you can be. To the other category of women in our TA who are already on their individual journey to self actualization, we are saying keep at it, beat the odds, be the best you can be despite challenges. As brand AXA we are instilling in women the confidence to progress,by showing our support towards their development and encouraging them whilst protecting what matters the most to them.

Campaign Framework

Introductory Video.
Introductory Radio
Reveal of IG live IWD panelist
Call for entry for #BeatTheOddsChooseToChallenge, an online challenge
Release of IWD visuals

Brief: Utilizing AXA's Know you can philosophy, create Visuals ,Radio and Film script for AXA's Personal Accident Insurance Plan.
